\begin{abstract}
In this material, we provide the entire dataset with normalized wind-power production, and the simulation-based results corresponding to the forecast provider A. First, given the calibrated SDE Model 2 using a training dataset with 73 non-contiguous segments, each 24-hours long, we show simulated wind power production paths and empirical pointwise confidence bands for the wind power production in all the 74 non-contiguous segments of the testing dataset. Then, we show the results obtained by swapping the training dataset's roles and the testing dataset.
\end{abstract}
